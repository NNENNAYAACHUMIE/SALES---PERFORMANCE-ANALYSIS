## Project Description
This project is an interactive Sales Dashboard built in Microsoft Excel. The dashboard was created to analyze sales performance across different managers, stores, product categories, and months. It provides insights that help management make better business decisions.
## Problem Statement

The company generates sales from different stores and product categories, but it is difficult to quickly identify:

- The best-performing sales managers
- The highest-selling product categories
- Monthly sales trends
- Which store performs better

A dashboard was developed to solve these problems by presenting the data in a simple and interactive way.
## Objectives
The objectives of this project are to:

- Analyze sales performance.
- Identify the top-performing managers.
- Compare sales between stores.
- Monitor monthly sales trends.
- Compare sales across product categories.
- ## Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- Data Cleaning

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains sales records, including:

- Manager Name
- Store
- Product Category
- Sales Amount
- Month

---

## Dashboard Features

- Top 10 Managers by Sales
- Sales by Product Category
- Monthly Sales Analysis
- Store Comparison
- Interactive Slicers

---

## Key Findings

- Spencer Lee recorded the highest total sales.
- Computech generated more sales than MicroWorld.
- PC sales contributed the highest revenue.
- Sales reached their peak in April.
- Sales declined slightly after April.

---

## Recommendations

- Reward top-performing managers to encourage high performance.
- Increase marketing for high-selling products such as PCs.
- Investigate the reasons for the decline in sales after April.
- Improve sales strategies for underperforming product categories.
- Adopt dashboards for regular business reporting.

---

## Conclusion

The dashboard makes it easy to monitor sales performance and identify important business trends. It provides useful insights that can help managers make informed decisions and improve business performance.
